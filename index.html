08 — Structural Findings

Confirmed

1. Anticipatory decay begins 4-5 days before closures. Feb 9-13 volume dropped 49% vs baseline with zero markets closed. Asian participants wind down before holidays.

2. Structural non-Asian floor: ~$1.7-1.9B. Three consecutive LNY sessions held at this level. US retail, market makers, minor intl participation.

3. Crypto is the most Asia-dependent sector (78% drop). Korean and HK offshore retail drive overnight crypto/Bitcoin ETF flow.

4. Monday overnight is 2.3x Tuesday. Weekend order accumulation across APAC. Must control for this in cross-session analysis.

Open Questions

1. Recovery never reached baseline. $3.0B avg vs $6.3B. Tariff contamination (SCOTUS Feb 20, new tariffs Feb 24) prevents clean attribution.

2. Feb 2 anomaly ($11.3B). 2x the next highest session. Month-start effect? Earnings?

3. Pre-holiday decay mechanism. What triggers pullback before closures? Broker margins? Fund mandates?

4. Need clean natural experiment. Golden Week, Chuseok, National Day for uncontaminated on/off/on cycles.
